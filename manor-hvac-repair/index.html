New Builds, Prairie Sun, Race-Weekend Traffic

Manor has transformed from a small railroad town east of Austin into a growth corridor along US 290 toward Elgin. Shadowglen’s golf-course and residential pods define a big share of mid-2000s and newer homes—attic units with builder-grade filtration that load up dust from ongoing construction nearby.

Circuit of the Americas race and concert weekends south of town rearrange SH 130 / 290 traffic more than residents outside motorsports notice. We track those calendars so a Sunday no-cool call isn’t stuck behind outbound concert dumps.

Prairie lots with thin shade mean condensers face full western exposure; we see high-pressure shutdowns here earlier in the afternoon than in oak-covered northwest suburbs. Emergency response still targets about 1–2 hours when east Travis crews are rolling.
