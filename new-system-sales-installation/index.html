When Replacement Beats Another Repair

Many Central Texas systems from the 1990s and early 2000s limp through summer with compressor patches and coil rinses. At some point the math flips: a correctly sized new system costs less over five to ten years than emergency visits every July.

We walk you through age, repair history, refrigerant type, and comfort complaints like rooms that never dry out. If repair still wins, we’ll say so. If a new system is the better buy, we sell and install it.

Sized for Central Texas—Not a Rule of Thumb

Oversized equipment cools the thermostat too fast and shuts off before wringing humidity out of the air. Our installs start with a load evaluation: insulation, sun exposure, attic conditions, and square footage.
